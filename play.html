Chess

Chess has been an absorbing hobby of mine since 2021, ignited by countless evenings of friendly matches with my friend. Our matches, typically lasting from 4 pm to 10 pm, were characterized by amateur moves, often leaving queens, rooks, and even kings vulnerable for several turns.

If you share the same passion for chess, I invite you to join me on Chess.com. With a current ranking in the top 10% worldwide on the platform, I'm always eager to engage in stimulating matches with fellow enthusiasts. Feel free to challenge me; let's enjoy the thrill of this ancient game together.

Wikipedia

Delving into the realm of Wikipedia, my initial goal was to create articles in the fields of mathematics and technology. However, I found myself initially occupied with minor yet impactful tasks, primarily centered around providing crucial citations. This process quickly became an engaging pursuit for me.

Despite the seemingly modest nature of my contributions, I derive immense satisfaction from making subtle adjustments that often go unnoticed, yet serve to assist a select group of readers. It's the joy of these quiet, meaningful impacts that drives my dedication to the platform.

Movies and TV Shows

When it comes to movies, I'm really picky. I'd rather watch a movie/shows I've seen a billion times than start a new one. My most liked movies in no particular order would be Fight Club, The Dark Knight, and The Matrix.

The Dark Knight made me want to see all Christopher Nolan movies. I have seen a handful but keep procrastinating seeing the others. I enjoy watching Al Pacino perform, as well as Keanu Reeves.

When it comes to TV shows, I prefer the 10-season sitcoms. I've seen Modern Family, Seinfeld, Friends, The Big Bang Theory, and Brooklyn 99 all more than once.

Mary introduced me to the world of Nigerian movies, and I've been enjoying watching these local productions. She made a list which you can find here: Mary's List.

Music

According to my friends, my taste in music is quite unique. Billie Eilish has effortlessly captured my heart, leading me to listen to her entire discography up to five times this year (which, in my opinion, isn't excessive). Tracks like "TV," "All the good girls go to hell," and "Billie Bossa Nova" have become staples in my daily playlist.

I also hold a special place for Eminem, often finding his song "Mockingbird" frequently recommended in my daily Spotify mixes. My musical preferences further extend to include artists such as Melanie Martinez, Rihanna, Bruno Mars, and a diverse range of musicians.

Exploring local talents, I find myself drawn to the sounds of Asake and Mohbad (may his soul rest in peace), while my musical interests don't particularly align with artists like Davido and Wizkid, nor do I find myself engaged in conversations centered around them.

To delve deeper into my musical inclinations, I have compiled a variety of playlists featuring my most-played tracks. Feel free to explore my musical world on Spotify. You can also follow me, and I would be glad to reciprocate the gesture.

Nice and Paris Saint Germain, securing the treble both times.
